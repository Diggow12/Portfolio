Technologies

The Challenge

Logistics previously relied on manual methods: pickups were scheduled via email or WhatsApp with carriers, and spreadsheets were used to track everything. This caused numerous problems — incorrect information and operational errors — as cancellations and changes were not properly recorded.

As a result, information mismatches were constant — the operation would prepare for a pickup that was never going to happen, wasting time and resources. This was unacceptable during a high-demand period.

The Solution

The solution needed to be easy to use, deployed as quickly as possible, and work flawlessly. The automated tool we built did exactly that — providing reliability and standardizing the entire process, delivered in record time.

The solution has already secured the process for hundreds of collections and reduced information mismatches by 60%. Its success stems from being a simple solution to a complex problem.
